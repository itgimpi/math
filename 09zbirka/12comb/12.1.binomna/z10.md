10. resenje jednacine $\displaystyle \binom{n}{0} \binom{n}{1} +...+ \binom{n}{n} + log_{0.5}4 = 14$ je:

resenje:
<br>
$\displaystyle \binom{n}{0} \binom{n}{1} +...+ \binom{n}{n}$ jednako je sa $2^n$
<br> 
$log_{0.5}4 = log_{1/2}4 = log_{2^{-1}}4 = -log_{2}4 = -2$
<br> 
$2^n - 2 = 14$ 
<br>
$2^n = 16$
<br>
$n=4$
