<br>
$(A) -\displaystyle \frac{a^2+b^2}{a} ~~~~ (B)~ 2b ~~~~ (C)~ \displaystyle \frac{a^2+2ab-b^2}{a} ~~~~ (D)~ -2a ~~~~ (E)~ -\displaystyle \frac{a^2+2ab-b^2}{a}$.
